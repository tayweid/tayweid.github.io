Part 1 | Visualization, Probability, and Statistics

The first part of the class starts with a refresh/introduction of core probability and statistics concepts, implementing them using data in Excel, then using English, tables, and figures to communicate the data. Topics include random variables, conditional probability, sampling, normal distribution, and a simple model of causal relationships.
