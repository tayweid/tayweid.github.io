Part B | How Competitive Markets Work

// prices can coordinate buyers and sellers //

Part B reveals how markets solve the coordination problem from Part A. Through the elegant dance of supply and demand, prices emerge to coordinate economic activity and guide society toward efficiency. These aren't just abstract curvesâ€”they're the invisible forces that determine what gets produced, who gets what, and how resources flow through the economy. By the end of Part B, you'll understand why competitive markets are one of humanity's greatest innovations for creating prosperity.
