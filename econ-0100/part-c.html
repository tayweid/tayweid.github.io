// externalized costs and benefits lead to inefficient markets //

Part B showed us how markets coordinate perfectly when conditions are right, delivering efficient outcomes that maximize total surplus. But what happens when those perfect conditions break down? You'll discover how pollution, taxes, and corrective policies can either harm or heal market outcomes.
