## ECON 0150 Final Project | Rubric

#### Rubric for Paper

- Does it have a clear question? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *Is the question stated in a clear and simple way?*
- Does it summarize the data? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *Is it clear what the data looks like, shown through visualization or summarization of some kind?*
- Does it have correct and appropriate statistical analysis? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *Is the statistical test appropriate for the question?*
- Does it have a figure visualizing the main question/results? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *For example, is there a scatterplot with a regression line for a simple OLS model?*
- Does it mention OLS assumptions even indirectly? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *For example, is there mention of residual or autocorrelation plots?*
- Does it present findings correctly and clearly? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *Is it easy to understand the results of the statistical model in English without needing to interpret the betas?*
- Is the paper close to 2 pages? YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *If it's 1 or 2 pages, YES. If it's 3 or more substantive pages, SOMEWHAT. If longer, NO.*
- Does the paper use a Part 3 or 4 concept? YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *For example, did they run OLS, regression, or a t-test?*

- Does the paper report the model results clearly? EXCEPTIONAL (3) / YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *For example, 2 if they say their beta estimates in words, and 1 if they just report their betas.*

- Does the paper use a Part 5 concept? YES (2) / SOMEWHAT (1) / NO (0)
  - *For example, did they use categorical or numberical control variables, or interaction terms in their model?*
